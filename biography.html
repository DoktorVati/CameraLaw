Anthony Camera, Esq.

Anthony Camera is admitted to practice law in the State of Colorado. He founded Camera Law, P.C. in 2022 with a focus on client-first practice. Anthony was awarded his Juris Doctor from the University of Denver Sturm College of Law, Order of the Coif, in 2021.

Prior to and during law school, he worked at one of Denver's most esteemed and recognized Bankruptcy law firms, Berken Cloyes P.C., for six years. He has been twice published in the Norton Annual Survey of Bankruptcy Law (2021 and 2022 editions). He was nominated for the Bankruptcy Student of the Year award in 2021.

Mr. Camera has represented clients at more than twenty criminal defense trials and has overseen the resolution of thousands of criminal defense cases. His criminal practice focuses on zealous defense.

Common to both areas of practice, Mr. Camera likes to stick up for the underdog. He dislikes bullies and fights aggressively for what he believes is right.
